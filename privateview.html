ABOUT

A Private View is an independant Belgian production company. The company produced or coproduced some forty feature films amongst which ZAGROS (2017), VINCENT AND THE END OF THE WORLD (2016), OXYGEN (2010) and MOSCOW, BELGIUM (2008).A Private View also produced the 26 episode animation series RUSTY, the drama series AMIGO'S and award-winning shorts such as NAWEWE and BAD HUNTER.Currently they are developing Anke Blondé's first feature FLOATING and a new animated series MISTER PAPER (26*7').
